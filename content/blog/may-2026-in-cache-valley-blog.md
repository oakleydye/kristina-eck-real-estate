## A Note from Kristina

The real estate market continues to shift as we move through spring, and one of the biggest factors right now is interest rates. While many expected rates to come down more quickly this year, they’ve remained a bit higher than anticipated— largely due to ongoing inflation pressures.

- Interest Rates Holding Steady
Mortgage rates remain just above 6%, keeping buyers more cautious but still active when the right home comes up.
- Why Rates Aren’t Dropping Faster
Higher energy prices are adding pressure to inflation, which slows the pace of rate cuts and keeps borrowing costs elevated.
- What We’re Seeing Locally
In Cache Valley, homes are still selling, but buyers are taking more time and being more selective.
- Opportunities for Buyers
With less competition than peak years, buyers have more room to negotiate and make confident decisions.
- Advice for Sellers
Pricing and presentation matter. Well-positioned homes are still moving, while overpriced homes are sitting longer.

If you have questions about what this means for you, our team is always here to help!
