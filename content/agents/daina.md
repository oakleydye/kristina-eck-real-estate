Whether you’re looking for your first home in Box Elder County or a mountain retreat in Cache Valley, you need a realtor who knows these backroads by heart. I’m not just your agent; I’m your neighbor!

Growing up in Brigham City and spending my summers in the stunning landscapes of Cache Valley, I developed a deep-rooted love for Northern Utah. Today, I’m a proud mom of three beautiful kids, fueled by equal parts caffeine and a passion for helping families find their perfect home.

With 4 years of real estate expertise and 4 years in property management, I bring a sharp, 360-degree perspective to every deal. But here’s the thing: I don’t do "transactions." I build friendships. I treat my clients like family, ensuring you feel supported, heard, and excited from the first showing to the final signature.

I have big goals for my little family, and nothing is more rewarding than helping you reach your own milestones along the way. Let’s grab a coffee and turn your real estate dreams into a reality!
