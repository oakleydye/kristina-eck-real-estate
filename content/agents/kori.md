Kori brings organization, creativity, and thoughtful attention to detail to the Kristina Eck Team. As the team’s assistant and transaction coordinator, she helps ensure every step of the buying and selling process runs smoothly behind the scenes. From managing contracts and deadlines to supporting marketing and client communication, Kori plays an important role in creating a seamless experience for both clients and the team.

Born and raised in Cache Valley, Kori has an appreciation for the community and the place she has always called home. She recently completed her degree in Marketing, bringing a fresh perspective and creativity to the team’s branding and marketing efforts.

Outside of work, Kori enjoys being active, spending time outdoors, traveling, and hanging out with her golden retriever. She is passionate about health and wellness, loves creating and designing, and enjoys using social media as a way to connect.

It also helps that she’s Kristina’s niece! Growing up around Kristina’s business, Kori has always admired the care, trust, and dedication that go into helping people find a place to call home. She’s proud to be part of the team!
